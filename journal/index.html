Journal

Review trades by evidence, not emotion.

Use this journal structure for every VSA observation and every trade review.

Before Trade

  • What is the background?
  • What signal appeared?
  • What is the confirmation?
  • Where is invalidation?
  • What is the risk?

After Trade

  • Was the thesis valid?
  • Did I wait?
  • Did I move stop?
  • Was the mistake technical or emotional?
  • What will I repeat or remove?
